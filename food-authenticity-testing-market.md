<p><p>The food authenticity testing market encompasses various methods to verify the integrity and source of food products. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) techniques detect specific DNA sequences, ensuring species identification. LC-MS (Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry) analyzes chemical compounds for accurate profiling of food ingredients. Isotope methods measure variations in isotopes to trace origins and processes. Immunoassays utilize antibodies to detect specific proteins or allergens. Other methods include novel technologies like NMR and spectroscopic techniques, enhancing food verification processes across industries.</p></p>
