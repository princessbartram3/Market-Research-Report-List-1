<p><p>The electric garage heater market offers three main types: fan-forced, infrared, and ceramic heaters. </p><p>Fan-forced heaters utilize a fan to distribute warm air quickly, providing immediate heat. Infrared heaters emit radiant heat, warming objects and people directly without heating the air, making them efficient for larger spaces. Ceramic heaters use ceramic plates to generate heat, offering a compact design and quick warm-up time. Each type caters to different heating needs, preferences, and garage configurations, influencing consumer choices in the market.</p></p>
