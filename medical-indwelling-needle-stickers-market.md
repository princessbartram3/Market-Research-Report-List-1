<p><p>The medical indwelling needle stickers market is categorized into two main types: roll type and piece type. Roll type stickers are designed for ease of use, providing multiple stickers on a continuous roll, making them convenient for medical staff during application. In contrast, piece type stickers are individual units, allowing for targeted application in specific areas without the need for a dispenser. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing the safety and organization of indwelling needle placements in clinical settings.</p></p>
