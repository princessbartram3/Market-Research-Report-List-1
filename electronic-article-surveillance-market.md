<p><strong>The Electronic Article Surveillance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hard Tag</li><li>Soft Tag</li><li>Deactivator or Detacher</li><li>Detection System</li><li>Permanent Deactivation Tag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) market encompasses various types of security components. Hard tags are durable, often used for high-value items, while soft tags are more versatile and can be applied to a range of products. Deactivators or detachers remove the security feature at the point of sale, preventing alarms during exit. Detection systems scan for active tags at store exits, and permanent deactivation tags are designed for single-use, rendering them inactive post-purchase to prevent theft effectively.</p></p>
