<p><p>The body cleansers market is divided into acidic and alkalic types, each catering to different skin needs. Acidic body cleansers typically have a lower pH, helping to maintain the skin's natural barrier and are ideal for preventing dryness and irritation. Conversely, alkalic body cleansers feature a higher pH, which can effectively remove dirt and oils but may disrupt the skin's balance if used excessively. Both types aim to provide targeted skin benefits while ensuring cleanliness and comfort.</p></p>
