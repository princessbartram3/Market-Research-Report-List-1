<p><p>The Wireless Tire Monitor market includes two main types: Built-in and External. Built-in monitors are integrated directly into the tire or wheel system, offering seamless functionality and a cleaner aesthetic. They are often factory-installed and provide real-time data on tire pressure and temperature. External monitors, on the other hand, are attached to the tire valve or wheel externally. They are more affordable, easy to install, and portable, making them a popular choice for aftermarket use. Both types enhance vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
