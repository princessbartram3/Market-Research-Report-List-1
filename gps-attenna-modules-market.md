<p><strong>The GPS Attenna Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active GPS Attenna Module</li><li>Passive GPS Attenna Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS antenna module market is primarily divided into active and passive types. Active GPS antenna modules incorporate an integrated amplifier, enhancing signal reception and allowing for improved performance in challenging environments. These modules are ideal for applications requiring high sensitivity. Conversely, passive GPS antenna modules lack built-in amplification, relying on the external receiver to process signals. They are generally simpler and more cost-effective, making them suitable for applications with less demanding signal requirements. Both types cater to diverse market needs.</p></p>
