<p><p>Water-cooled centrifugal chillers are advanced cooling systems that use water as a cooling medium to remove heat from industrial or commercial spaces. These chillers operate on the principle of centrifugal force, where a rotating impeller compresses refrigerant gas, which is then condensed using water in a heat exchanger. They are highly efficient, suitable for large-scale applications, and widely used in industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, and data centers due to their ability to handle high cooling loads with lower energy consumption.</p><p>The Water-Cooled Centrifugal Chillers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.7% during the forecast period.
