<p><p>Bi-metallic composite bearings consist of a steel backing and a layer of a bronze material, which can be either leaded or lead-free. The steel + lead bronze powder type combines steel with lead bronze for enhanced wear resistance and strength. In contrast, steel + lead-free bronze powder offers an environmentally friendly alternative, suitable for applications requiring reduced toxicity while maintaining durability. Both materials ensure effective load-bearing capabilities, reduced friction, and extended service life in various industrial applications.</p></p>
