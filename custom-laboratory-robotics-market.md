<p><strong>The Custom Laboratory Robotics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic Robot</li><li>Semi-Automatic Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The custom laboratory robotics market is divided into two primary types: fully automatic robots and semi-automatic robots. Fully automatic robots operate independently, handling all tasks without human intervention, making them ideal for high-throughput environments that require consistent precision and efficiency. Conversely, semi-automatic robots assist human operators in certain processes, combining automation with manual control. This type offers flexibility and adaptability, enabling laboratories to streamline workflows while allowing for human oversight when necessary. Both types enhance productivity in laboratory settings.</p></p>
