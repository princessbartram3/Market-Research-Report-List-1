<p><p>The automotive speed encoder market is segmented into axial and radial encoders, each serving distinct applications. Axial encoders measure rotational speed along the axis of rotation, making them suitable for precise applications like wheel speed monitoring. In contrast, radial encoders measure speed at a right angle to the axis, commonly used in applications like motor control and steering systems. Both types enhance vehicle performance and safety by providing accurate speed data for various automotive functionalities.</p></p>
