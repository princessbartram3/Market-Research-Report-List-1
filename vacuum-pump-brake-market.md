<p><p>The Vacuum Pump Brake market includes two primary types: Mechanical and Electric. Mechanical vacuum pumps are driven by the engine via a belt or pulley system, offering reliability and cost-effectiveness for traditional vehicles. Electric vacuum pumps, powered by an electric motor, are increasingly popular in modern and hybrid vehicles due to their energy efficiency, compact design, and ability to operate independently of the engine. Both types enhance braking performance by generating vacuum pressure for brake boosters.</p></p>
