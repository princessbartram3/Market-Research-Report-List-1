<p><p>The fixed optical mount market is categorized into cage mountable and post mountable types. Cage mountable mounts are designed to integrate seamlessly into optical cage systems, providing versatility and stability for various optical components. These mounts allow for precise alignment and adjustment within a modular framework. In contrast, post mountable options are suited for standalone or fixed installations, utilizing posts to secure optical elements in place. Both types cater to different applications, ensuring accurate positioning for optical experiments and setups.</p></p>
