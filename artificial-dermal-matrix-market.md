<p><ul><li>From Animal Source</li><li>From Human Source</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Artificial Dermal Matrix market is categorized into two primary types based on the source: animal-derived and human-derived matrices. Animal-source matrices, often made from porcine or bovine tissues, provide structural support and promote healing while minimizing rejection. Human-source matrices, derived from donated human skin, offer enhanced biocompatibility and reduced immunogenicity. Both types serve as scaffolds for skin regeneration in wound care, burns, and reconstructive surgeries, catering to varying patient needs and preferences in clinical applications.</p></p>
