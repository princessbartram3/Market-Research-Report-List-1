<p><p>The biomedical pressure sensors market comprises several types, including capacitive, fiber optic, telemetric, and piezoresistive sensors. Capacitive sensors measure pressure changes by sensing variations in capacitance. Fiber optic sensors utilize light transmission to detect pressure fluctuations, offering high accuracy and resistance to electromagnetic interference. Telemetric sensors wirelessly transmit pressure data for remote monitoring. Piezoresistive sensors operate based on the change in electrical resistance when pressure is applied. Additional sensor types may include optical and strain gauge technologies, broadening the market's applications.</p></p>
