<p><ul><li>Open Loop</li><li>Closed Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Prepaid and gift cards function in two primary market types: open loop and closed loop. Open loop cards can be used at multiple retailers and locations, as they are linked to major payment networks like Visa or Mastercard. In contrast, closed loop cards are restricted to specific retailers or brands, making them usable only at designated outlets. Both types offer convenience, but open loop cards provide greater flexibility, while closed loop cards often offer exclusive promotions or rewards within their network.</p></p>
