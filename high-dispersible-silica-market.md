<p><p>The high dispersible silica market consists of two main types: granular and micropearl. Granular high dispersible silica features larger particle sizes and is often used in applications requiring bulk handling, such as rubber production and coatings. In contrast, micropearls are finer and offer enhanced flowability and dispersion in formulations, making them suitable for cosmetics and personal care products. Both types serve distinct purposes across various industries, contributing to improved performance characteristics in end products.</p></p>
