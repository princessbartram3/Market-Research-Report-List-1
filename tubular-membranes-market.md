<p><p>Tubular membranes are used in various filtration processes, with Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) and Reverse Osmosis (RO) being key market types. MBR combines biological treatment with membrane filtration, effectively removing contaminants and producing high-quality effluent, ideal for wastewater treatment. RO uses semi-permeable membranes to separate impurities from water, primarily for desalination and purification. Both technologies leverage tubular membranes for durability and efficiency, catering to industries like municipal water treatment, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals, addressing global water scarcity and quality challenges.</p></p>
