<p><p>The tow tractor market is categorized into three main types: pedestrian towing tractors, stand-in towing tractors, and rider-seated towing tractors. Pedestrian towing tractors are designed for manual operation, allowing users to walk behind while towing. Stand-in towing tractors provide a platform for operators to stand while maneuvering loads, enhancing visibility and control. Rider-seated towing tractors accommodate operators sitting during operation, offering comfort for longer tasks and more extensive load handling in warehouses or industrial settings.</p></p>
