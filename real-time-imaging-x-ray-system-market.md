<p><p>The Real-Time Imaging X-Ray System market is primarily categorized into two types: Flat Panel Detector (FPD) technology and Linear Array Detector technology. FPDs utilize an integrated digital sensor to capture high-resolution images quickly, providing efficient and detailed diagnostics with reduced radiation exposure. In contrast, Linear Array Detectors utilize a series of elongated sensors arranged in a line to capture image data incrementally; they are particularly advantageous for specific applications requiring rapid scanning and more straightforward setup in clinical environments.</p></p>
