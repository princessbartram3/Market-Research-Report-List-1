<p><p>In the shopping eGift card market, there are two primary types: open-loop and closed-loop cards. Open-loop cards can be used across multiple retailers and platforms, functioning like prepaid debit cards. They offer flexibility for consumers to choose where to spend. In contrast, closed-loop cards are restricted to specific stores or brands, allowing for targeted promotions and customer loyalty. Each type serves different consumer needs, with open-loop prioritizing versatility and closed-loop focusing on brand loyalty.</p></p>
