<p><p>Automotive rear-view reversing cameras enhance safety by providing a clear view of the rear while reversing. The market primarily features two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras offer superior image quality, especially in low-light conditions, making them ideal for premium vehicles. CMOS cameras are more cost-effective, energy-efficient, and compact, suitable for mass-market vehicles. Both types are widely used, with CCD cameras excelling in performance and CMOS cameras dominating due to affordability and technological advancements in image processing.</p></p>
