<p><p>The digital tracking receiver market is categorized into single-channel and multi-channel systems. In contrast, multi-channel receivers can simultaneously process multiple signals, offering enhanced capabilities for complex environments and applications such as telecommunications, navigation, and surveillance. This differentiation caters to varying user needs, from basic tracking tasks to advanced scenario management.</p></p>
