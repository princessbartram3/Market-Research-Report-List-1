<p><strong>The Colored Concrete Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acid-based Staining</li><li>Water-based Staining</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The colored concrete market comprises primarily two types: acid-based staining and water-based staining. Acid-based staining reacts chemically with the concrete, creating rich, translucent hues that enhance the surface's natural characteristics, offering a durable finish ideal for outdoor applications. In contrast, water-based staining involves pigments that saturate the concrete's surface, providing a broader range of colors and easier application with quick drying times. Both methods enhance aesthetics and durability, catering to diverse design preferences and project needs.</p></p>
