<p><p>The high-speed dispenser market is categorized into fully automatic and semi-automatic types. Fully automatic dispensers operate without human intervention, offering greater efficiency and speed, making them ideal for high-volume applications. They incorporate advanced technologies like sensors and automation systems. In contrast, semi-automatic dispensers require some human involvement, allowing for flexibility and control at a lower cost. They are commonly used in medium-sized operations where speed is important, but complete automation isn't feasible or necessary.</p></p>
