<p><p>The CNC machine tool blade market encompasses various types of cutting tools essential for precision machining processes. Turning tool blades are designed for shaping materials by rotating them against a fixed cutting tool. Drilling tool blades create holes in materials using rotational movement, often featuring specialized designs for efficiency. Milling tool blades, on the other hand, remove material from a workpiece through rotary motion, allowing for complex shapes and finishes. Together, these tools are vital for various manufacturing applications.</p></p>
