<p><p>Hay and forage baling equipment is essential for agricultural operations, facilitating efficient harvest and storage of forage. The main types include round balers and square balers. Round balers produce cylindrical bales, which are easier to handle, transport, and store, particularly for larger fields. Square balers, on the other hand, create rectangular bales that can be stacked more efficiently, making them ideal for smaller operations and precise storage.
